#Dashboard Highlights
1. Month-Wise Total Sales
Visualizes monthly fluctuations in revenue and identifies peak sales months.
2. Product & Subscription Type Sales
Shows total sales distribution across Microsoft products like Azure, Teams, Xbox, Office 365, GitHub, Windows, Dynamics 365, etc.
3. Industry-Wise Satisfaction
Displays satisfaction or contract duration across industries such as Manufacturing, Technology, Retail, Finance, etc.
4. Region-Wise Sales
Compares sales across global regions including North America, Europe, Asia, Middle East, Australia, and Africa.

#Tools Used
  Tableau Desktop 
  Excel (Data Cleaning & Preparation)

#Key Insights
1.Asia generated the highest revenue among all regions.
2.Azure, Office 365, and Windows performed exceptionally well.
3.Most industries showed increasing satisfaction or contract duration.
4.Sales peaked in April and July, showing seasonal buying patterns.
